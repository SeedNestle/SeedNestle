Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

Known for its air-purifying qualities, the Spider Plant is a resilient and low-maintenance indoor favorite.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

With its elegant white flowers, the Peace Lily is known for purifying indoor air and adding a touch of serenity.

Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

This trailing vine is a popular choice for beginners due to its ability to grow in a variety of lighting conditions.

Snake Plant (Sansevieria trifasciata)

The Snake Plant is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in low light and improves air quality.

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

Known for its glossy, dark green leaves, the ZZ Plant is nearly indestructible and thrives in low light.

Ficus (Ficus elastica)

The Ficus is a popular indoor plant with glossy, bold leaves that make a striking addition to any space.

Philodendron

Philodendrons are known for their beautiful, heart-shaped leaves and are incredibly easy to care for in indoor environments.

Monstera (Monstera deliciosa)

The Monstera, with its iconic split leaves, adds a tropical feel to indoor spaces and grows quickly.

English Ivy (Hedera helix)

English Ivy is an adaptable climbing vine that thrives in a variety of indoor environments, improving air quality.
